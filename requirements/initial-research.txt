I looked at both commercial platforms and open‑source projects to see how youth football clubs handle attendance and tournament sign‑ups. The research shows that mainstream team‑management apps (e.g., Teamy, Spond, TeamSnap and Save This Date) provide robust scheduling, RSVP and attendance tracking features, but they require users to sign up through apps or websites and do not parse free‑form WhatsApp messages. To preserve the simplicity of unstructured WhatsApp communication, a custom solution would need to combine a WhatsApp integration layer (such as the self‑hosted WAHA API) with an open‑source chatbot and workflow engine like BuilderBot and n8n. These components allow you to receive and send WhatsApp messages via an API, parse keywords, and automate reminders and notifications. No complete self‑hosted tool exists today, but building one is feasible by using these building blocks.
