# Surveys & Questionnaires Guide

> For coaches who want to collect feedback, Trikot orders, or other information from parents.

## Quick Start

1. Go to the coach dashboard
2. Click **Surveys > New Survey**
3. Choose a template or start from scratch
4. Share the link in your WhatsApp group

## Ready-Made Templates

### Trikot & Cap Order

Collects per player:
- Trikot size (children's: 116, 128, 140, 152, 164 / adult: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL)
- Cap size (adjustable, S–M, L–XL)
- Name or number for the back (optional)

The results page shows a **quantity summary per size** — ready to send to the supplier.

If payments are enabled, you can attach prices to each item. Parents pay after submitting.

### End-of-Semester Feedback

Anonymous by default. Collects:
- Overall satisfaction (5-star rating)
- Training quality (5-star rating)
- Communication quality (5-star rating)
- "What should we improve?" (free text)
- "What did you enjoy most?" (free text)

Results show **average ratings** and all text responses (without names).

## Creating a Custom Survey

### Question Types

| Type | Use for |
|------|---------|
| **Single choice** | Yes/No questions, pick one option |
| **Multiple choice** | Select all that apply |
| **Star rating** | Satisfaction, quality (1–5 stars) |
| **Free text** | Comments, suggestions |
| **Size picker** | Clothing sizes (pre-filled dropdown) |

### Anonymous vs. Identified

When creating a survey, choose:

- **Anonymous**: No names attached to responses. You see aggregated results only. Cannot send reminders (because you don't know who responded).
- **Identified**: Responses are linked to the player's nickname. You can see who responded and send reminders to those who haven't. Names are still only nicknames — no phone numbers or real names are shown.

## Sharing the Survey

Every survey gets:
- A **unique URL** — paste it anywhere (WhatsApp, Signal, email, website)
- A **QR code** — print it or display on screen

Just share the link in your WhatsApp group chat. Parents open it on their phone, fill it in, done.

## Deadlines & Reminders

- Set a **response deadline** when creating the survey
- For identified surveys, OpenKick sends **WhatsApp reminders** to parents who haven't responded
- Anonymous surveys cannot send reminders (there's no way to know who hasn't responded)

## Viewing Results

Go to **Surveys > [Your Survey] > Results** to see:
- Star ratings as averages with bar charts
- Size distributions as summary tables
- Free-text responses listed individually
- For identified surveys: who responded and who didn't

## Closing a Survey

Click **Close Survey** to stop accepting responses. The results remain accessible. Closed surveys move to the archive.
