# Quick Start for Parents

Welcome to OpenKick! This guide helps you get started in under 2 minutes.

## How It Works

Your club uses a WhatsApp number to manage training attendance and tournament sign-ups. You can talk to it just like you would text a coach — no app to install, no account to create.

## Step 1: Join Your Club

Your coach will share either:

- **A QR code** — open your phone camera, scan it, and it opens a WhatsApp chat automatically.
- **A link** — tap it and it opens a WhatsApp chat with the club's number.

## Step 2: Register Your Child

When you message the club number for the first time, it will ask for your child's name (or nickname). Just type it. That's the only information you need to provide.

Example:
> **You:** Luca
> **Bot:** Got it! Luca is registered. You'll get reminders before trainings and tournaments.

If you have multiple children in the club, just add them one by one when asked.

## Step 3: Report Attendance

Before each training, you'll get a reminder. You can reply in your own words — no special format needed:

| What you write | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| "Luca is coming" | Marked as attending |
| "Luca sick" | Marked as absent |
| "Luca will be late, around 18:30" | Marked as attending (late) |
| "Not coming this week" | Marked as absent |
| "We're on holiday until March 5" | Marked as absent for all sessions until then |

You'll get a short confirmation after each message so you know it was received.

## Step 4: Sign Up for Tournaments

When a tournament is announced, you'll get a WhatsApp message with details (date, location, deadline). To register:

| What you write | What happens |
| --- | --- |
| "Luca is in for the tournament" | Registered |
| "Sign up Luca" | Registered |
| "Cancel Luca for Saturday's tournament" | Registration removed |

If the tournament is full, Luca goes on a waiting list and you'll be notified if a spot opens up.

## Using the Website (Optional)

Your club also has a simple website where you can:

- See upcoming trainings and tournaments
- Check your child's attendance history
- Sign up or cancel for events
- Download tournament info (PDF files with rules, directions, etc.)

Your coach will share the website link. No login needed for basic features — just open it on your phone.

## Tips

- **You don't need to install anything.** Everything works through WhatsApp and an optional website.
- **You can write naturally.** The system understands casual messages. No commands to memorise.
- **Reminders are automatic.** You'll get a nudge before deadlines so you don't forget.
- **Your data is minimal.** Only your child's name and your phone number are stored. No email, no password, no personal details.

## Need Help?

- Message the club's WhatsApp number with "help" and it will explain what you can do.
- Talk to your coach — they can check and update attendance manually if needed.
